These faith-based spells provide players with offensive, defensive, and useful effects that offer great flexibility in overcoming the game’s many challenges. In terms of storage, Elden Ring departs from FromSoftware’s previous game, Dark Souls 3, by forgoing attunement and instead allowing players to collect unique items to increase the number of summons and spells they can equip.

The summons in the Elden Ring are reminiscent of the wonders and pyromancers from previous FromSoftware games like Dark Souls. They work similarly to spells, but use faith instead of intelligence. Although summons offer many offensive options, they are very different from spells in that they have the ability to restore health and cure illness. Following are the steps to use enchantments in Elden Ring.

How to use enchantments in Elden Ring Before you start getting and using enchantments, you will need to equip a holy seal in the Elden Ring. Holy Seals are similar to weapons and enchantments are ammunition for that weapon. There are different Holy Seals that you can buy or get after completing an objective. Like enchantments, seals are also related to the attributes of faith. Head to the Roundtable Hold to buy a Seal and an Enchantment. You can buy a Finger Seal from Twin Maiden Husks for 800 runes in the Roundtable Cellar. You can buy enchantments from Brother Corhyn. Now that you have the Holy Seal and the enchantments, all you need to do is head to the Site of Grace. When you rest on the site, you will get different options in the menu. Select Memorize Spell to equip an enchantment to a memory slot. Next, select the Gear section and equip a Holy Seal. Once you have a sigil equipped, you can conjure and cast an enchantment.
